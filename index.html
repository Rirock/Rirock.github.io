About

Jiayi Li is an Assistant Professor at Ritsumeikan University. His research interests include AI for drug discovery, molecular property prediction, activity cliffs, multimodal molecular representation learning, brain-inspired neural networks, and large language models.

Research Interests

  • AI for drug discovery and molecular property prediction
  • Activity cliffs and complex structure-property relationships
  • Multimodal molecular representation learning with SMILES, graphs, and fingerprints
  • Brain-inspired neural networks and computational intelligence
  • Large language models for scientific discovery
