About

Jiayi Li is an Assistant Professor at Ritsumeikan University. His research interests include brain-inspired neural networks and their applications in medical diagnosis and drug discovery, as well as large language models and computational intelligence.

Research Interests

  • Brain-inspired neural networks and their applications in medical diagnosis and drug discovery
  • Large language models and computational intelligence
  • Molecular property prediction and multimodal molecular representation learning
